## Key Functions in script.js

- **createDeck**: Creates a standard 52-card deck.
- **shuffleDeck**: Randomly shuffles the cards.
- **placeBet**: Places a bet and updates the bankroll.
- **handleDeal**: Starts the game and deals the initial cards.
- **handleHit**: Adds a card to the player's hand.
- **handleStand**: Ends the player's turn and lets the dealer play.
- **calculateHand**: Calculates the total value of a hand, accounting for Aces.
